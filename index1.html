About Us

Neville Longbottom Liberty Law Firm is a Trinidad based multi-faceted law firm that focuses mainly on Family law and Personal injury law but is also equipped to handle a variety of cases.
Liberty law was established in August 2008 after Neville resigned from Oxfords and Son law firm in London. He was employed there for three years before he made a name for himself and decided to branch off on his own. Neville, who has roots in Trinidad because of his mother, decided to open his practice here. However, he still gets clients from around the world. Neville attribute this to his attention to detail, approachable demeanour and passion for his work and wanting to help others.
Neville brought in Ronald Weasley just under two years of the firm being established, as his clientele was growing. They introduced child counselling and support soon after for the children in surrounding communities. They changed the minds of the people in the community and made counselling sessions less of a taboo. Hermoine Granger was brought in on cases when a psychologist was required and her love for the people and the island made her stay. And four years later, Communication major, Ginny Weasley, was brought in, thus completing the team that you see before you.
The team has worked on numerous cases, some being purely pro bono as the law firm has a love for helping their clients more than getting a paycheck. The law firm has never lost a case, whether nationally or internationally. They have gotten cases where agreements were settled outside of the court and no client has ever walked away unsatisfied. This makes Liberty Law firm a top ranking law firm, right up there with internal firms.
